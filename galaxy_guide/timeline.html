Star Wars Movie Timeline

The Star Wars story spans over thousands of years, although most of the movies cover a few decade long period. There are many ways to watch the Star Wars movies, but the graphic below can give some guidance on when everything is taking place. (B/ABY means Before/After the Battle of Yavin, a key event in A New Hope)

The Phantom Menace - 32 BBY

Two Jedi sent to settle a galactic dispute discover a young boy, Anakin Skywalker, who is incredibly gifted in the Force.

Attack of the Clones - 22 BBY

A now grown Anakin Skywalker is reunited with his forbidden love, Padme, as they find themselves thrown in the middle of a galactic conflict.

Revenge of the Sith - 19 BBY

Near the end of the Clone Wars, desperate to save his wife Padme, Anakin is slowly seduced by the power of the dark side.

Solo: A Star Wars Story - 10 BBY

Years before joining the Rebel Alliance, a young Han Solo makes his way through the galaxy, getting involved in high-risk criminal enterprises.

Rogue One: A Star Wars Story - 1 BBY

The Rebel Alliance sends a daring group of heroes, who attempt to steal plans for the Death Star; the Galactic Empireâ€™s secret planet-destroying weapon.

A New Hope - 0 BBY

Luke Skywalker is unknowingly drawn into the Galactic Civil War, discovering that he holds a mysterious and unknown power.

The Empire Strikes Back - 3 ABY

After the destruction of the Death Star, Luke seeks further enlightenment and training in the ways of the force from an ancient Jedi Master.

Return of the Jedi - 4 ABY

As the Rebel Alliance struggles to destroy the second Death Star and finish off the Empire once and for all, Luke seeks to redeem Darth Vader from the dark side.
