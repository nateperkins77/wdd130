Star Wars Movie Timeline

The Star Wars story spans over thousands of years, although most of the movies cover a few decade long period. There are many ways to watch the Star Wars movies, but the graphic below can give some guidance on when everything is taking place.

The Phantom Menace

Two Jedi sent to settle a trade dispute discover a young boy, Anakin Skywalker, who is incredibly gifted in the Force.

Attack of the Clones

A now grown Anakin Skywalker is reunited with his forbidden love, Padme, as they find themselves thrown in the middle of a galactic conflict.

Revenge of the Sith

Nearing the end of the Clone Wars, Anakin is slowly seduced by the power of the dark side, desperate to save his wife Padme.

Solo: A Star Wars Story

Years before joining the Rebel Alliance, a young Han Solo makes his way through the galaxy with his future copilot Chewbacca.

Rogue One: A Star Wars Story

A New Hope

Luke Skywalker is unknowingly drawn into the Galactic Civil War, discovering that he holds a mysterious and unknown power.

The Empire Strikes Back

After the destruction of the Death Star, Luke seeks further enlightenment and training in the ways of the force from an ancient Jedi Master.

Return of the Jedi

As the Rebel Alliance struggles to destroy the second Death Star and finish off the Empire once and for all, Luke tries to redeem Darth Vader from the dark side.
