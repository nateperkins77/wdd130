Characters

Star Wars has a diverse cast of heroes, villains, aliens, robots and more. With so many to keep track of, we have provided information on some of the most important characters below.

Luke Skywalker

Affiliation

Jedi

Bio

Raised on the desert planet Tatooine by his aunt and uncle, Luke Skywalker unexpectedly crosses paths with the Rebel Alliance and joins the fight against the evil Galactic Empire.

Key Appearances

A New Hope, Empire Strikes Back, Return of the Jedi, The Last Jedi

Quote

“I am a Jedi, like my father before me”

Han Solo

Affiliation

None

Bio

Rising from poverty to smuggler captain of the Millennium Falcon, Han Solo’s carefree attitude and sharp wit get him into trouble at every turn. But his resourcefulness and loyalty quickly become critical assets to the Rebel Alliance.

Key Appearances

A New Hope, Empire Strikes Back, Return of the Jedi, Solo: A Star Wars Story, The Force Awakens

Quote

“Never tell me the odds”

Leia Organa

Affiliation

Rebel Alliance

Bio

Born to be a politician, Leia is the adopted daughter of a senator from Alderaan. As a critical part of the Rebel Alliance, Leia helps lead the rebellion against Darth Vader and Emperor Palpatine.

Key Appearances

A New Hope, Empire Strikes Back, Return of the Jedi, The Force Awakens, The Last Jedi

Quote

"Aren't you a little short for a stormtrooper?"

Obi-Wan Kenobi

Affiliation

Jedi

Bio

As a wielder of the force, Obi-Wan Kenobi served on the Jedi High Council towards the end of the Galactic Republic. He is Luke’s Jedi mentor, teaching him in the ways of the force.

Key Appearances

The Phantom Menace, Attack of the Clones, Revenge of the Sith, A New Hope

Quote

“If you strike me down, I shall become more powerful than you can possibly imagine”

Darth Vader

Affiliation

Sith

Bio

Darth Vader is what remains of the once legendary Anakin Skywalker. Now the emperor’s apprentice, Darth Vader is a powerful wielder of the dark side of the Force. Where Vader goes, death and destruction follow.

Key Appearances

The Phantom Menace, Attack of the Clones, Revenge of the Sith, A New Hope, Empire Strikes Back, Return of the Jedi

Quote

“You have controlled your fear. Now release your anger. Only your hatred can destroy me”

Emperor Palpatine

Affiliation

Sith

Bio

Once a humble senator from Naboo, Sheev Palpatine went on to orchastrate the Galactic Civil War. After playing both sides, only Palpatine remained victorious, declaring himself Emperor of the Galaxy. Key Appearances

Key Appearances

The Phantom Menace, Attack of the Clones, Revenge of the Sith, Empire Strikes Back, Return of the Jedi

Quote

"All who gain power are afraid to lose it"
