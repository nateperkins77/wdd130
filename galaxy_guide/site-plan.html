Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help new fans learn about Star Wars and get caught up on the lore. This website will also serve as a news source for new and upcoming Star Wars projects.

Audience

The audience for this website is anyone who wants to learn more about Star Wars. New and old fans can find information on their favorite characters, upcoming Star Wars projects, and my opinions on past projects.

Content

Home page

Overview: Star Wars is the epitome of adventure. It’s the struggle between right and wrong, light and darkness, good and evil. Packed with epic characters, unique alien species, and mystical powers, Star Wars contains much more substance than your typical sci-fi flick. The stories found within Star Wars illustrate powerful motifs, mesmerizing visuals, and incredible moral conflicts. The lessons taught by Star Wars will last a lifetime, and the characters are worth looking up to. This website will focus primarily on Star Wars movies, but there is so much more out there for those willing to dive a little deeper. Purpose: Galaxy Guide was created to help new fans of Star Wars to learn about the world, the characters, and even the timeline of events. Designed for fans new and old alike, Galaxy Guide will help you “Explore the stories, characters, and lore of a galaxy far, far away”. From summaries to deep dives to my own personal opinions, use Galaxy Guide as a partner as you begin or continue your journey across the stars.

Characters

Luke Skywalker Affiliation: Jedi Bio: Raised on the desert planet Tatooine by his aunt and uncle, Luke Skywalker unexpectedly crosses paths with the Rebel Alliance and joins the fight against the evil Galactic Empire. Key Appearances: A New Hope, Empire Strikes Back, Return of the Jedi, The Last Jedi Fun Fact/Quote: “I am a Jedi, like my father before me” Han Solo Affiliation: None Bio: Rising from poverty to smuggler captain of the Millennium Falcon, Han Solo’s carefree attitude and sharp wit get him into trouble at every turn. But his resourcefulness and loyalty become critical assets to the Rebel Alliance. Key Appearances: A New Hope, Empire Strikes Back, Return of the Jedi, Solo: A Star Wars Story, The Force Awakens Fun Fact/Quote: “Never tell me the odds” Leia Organa Affiliation: Rebel Alliance Bio: Born to be a politician, Leia is the adopted daughter of a senator from Alderaan. As a critical part of the Rebel Alliance, Leia helps lead the rebellion against Darth Vader and Emperor Palpatine. Key Appearances: A New Hope, Empire Strikes Back, Return of the Jedi, The Force Awakens, The Last Jedi Fun Fact/Quote: Princess Leia became a senator for her planet as a teenager, which then led to her joining the rebellion. Obi-Wan Kenobi Affiliation: Jedi Bio: As a wielder of the force, Obi-Wan Kenobi served on the Jedi High Council towards the end of the Galactic Republic. He is Luke’s Jedi mentor, teaching him in the ways of the force. Key Appearances: The Phantom Menace, Attack of the Clones, Revenge of the Sith, A New Hope Fun Fact/Quote: “If you strike me down, I shall become more powerful than you can possibly imagine” Darth Vader Affiliation: Sith Bio: Darth Vader is what remains of the once legendary Anakin Skywalker. Now the emperor’s apprentice, Darth Vader is a powerful wielder of the dark side of the Force. Where Vader goes, death and destruction follow. Key Appearances: The Phantom Menace, Attack of the Clones, Revenge of the Sith, A New Hope, Empire Strikes Back, Return of the Jedi Fun Fact/Quote: “You have controlled your fear. Now release your anger. Only your hatred can destroy me”

Timeline

The Phantom Menace Two Jedi sent to settle a trade dispute discover a young boy, Anakin Skywalker, who is incredibly gifted in the Force. Attack of the Clones A now grown Anakin Skywalker is reunited with his forbidden love, Padme, as they find themselves thrown in the middle of a galactic conflict. Revenge of the Sith Nearing the end of the Clone Wars, Anakin is slowly seduced by the power of the dark side, desperate to save his wife Padme. Solo: A Star Wars Story Years before joining the Rebel Alliance, a young Han Solo makes his way through the galaxy with his future copilot Chewbacca. Rogue One The Rebel Alliance sends a daring group of heroes who attempt to steal plans for the Death Star, the Galactic Empire’s secret planet-destroying weapon. A New Hope Luke Skywalker is unknowingly drawn into the Galactic Civil War, discovering that he holds a mysterious and unknown power. Empire Strikes Back After the destruction of the Death Star, Luke seeks further enlightenment and training in the ways of the force from an ancient Jedi Master. Return of the Jedi As the Rebel Alliance struggles to destroy the second Death Star and finish off the Empire once and for all, Luke tries to redeem Darth Vader from the dark side.
